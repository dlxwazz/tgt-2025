Legal Responsibilities & Regulatory Compliance

At The Gambler Network, we operate differently from traditional centralized casinos. Our platform is decentralized, governed by collective consensus rather than a single entity.

  • Decentralized & Fair: The Gambler Network has no single owner—our ecosystem is shaped by the community.
  • No House Edge: Our team does not take any margin from player winnings or losses.
  • Dynamic Rewards: We do not guarantee fixed rewards. Payouts are distributed in $tGt, with values fluctuating based on the liquidity pool.
  • Transparent & Open-Source: All game algorithms and smart contract codes are publicly accessible, ensuring fairness and security.
  • Blockchain-Powered Compliance: Our chance-based games align with international standards, offering fully auditable and verifiable gameplay.
